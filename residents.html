Electronics Recycling for Residents

We make it easy for homeowners throughout New England to responsibly dispose of their old electronics. Our residential recycling services ensure your devices are handled securely and environmentally responsibly.

What We Accept

  • Desktop and laptop computers
  • Monitors and displays
  • Printers and scanners
  • Keyboards, mice, and accessories
  • Mobile phones and tablets
  • TVs and entertainment systems

Data Security

We understand the importance of protecting your personal information. All devices containing storage media receive secure data destruction services to protect your privacy.

Convenient Drop-off

Multiple convenient locations throughout New England make it easy to drop off your electronics for recycling. Contact us to find the nearest location.
